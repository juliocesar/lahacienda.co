About us

La Hacienda Serviços de Tecnologia da Informação LTDA is a software studio based in Brazil. We design and build our own applications end to end — from the computational engine at their core to the interface people use every day.

We work in TypeScript across mobile and web, and we favor software that is fast, respects privacy, and keeps working without a network. Our apps do their computation on the device rather than on servers, so they stay useful offline and collect nothing about the people who use them.

Products

cecco

An astrological almanac and ritual companion · iOS & Android

Given a moment and a place, cecco reports the planetary day and the planetary hour, the moon’s phase, its zodiac sign, and the lunar mansion — the traditional coordinates of time used in classical astrology and magic. From those it suggests rituals, incenses, psalms, and prayers historically considered appropriate to the hour.

Everything cecco shows is computed locally from the date, time, and location you give it. It needs no account, shows no ads, and works fully offline. cecco presents its corpus as historical and educational material.

Currently in development; launching on the App Store and Google Play.
