Privacy Policy

cecco

cecco does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device and works offline.

cecco computes everything it shows — the planetary day and hour, the moon’s phase, sign, and mansion, and the content it suggests — locally on your device from the date, time, and location you provide. None of this leaves your device.

We do not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or third-party SDKs that collect data. We have no servers that receive your information, and we do not create user accounts.

If you grant cecco access to your device location, it is used only on your device to compute your local astrological reading and is never transmitted or stored off-device.

Because we collect nothing, there is no personal data for us to share, sell, or delete on request.
